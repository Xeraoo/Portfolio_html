About me

Let me introduce myself.

    When I was a young child, I was always interested in fairy tales that presented a world that was foreign to the main character, but in order to find my way in it he always had a map with him as his signpost. This is how a passion for maps was born to me. Over time and learning about new technologies, thanks to choosing Geospatial Informatics as my educational path in college, I fell in love with mapmaking, environmental data analysis and all kinds of data analysis about our planet.

    For several years now, I have been eager to learn about new technologies that allow me to create modern geospatial products that have implications in the real world. Some of the presented projects were created as part of assignments during my studies, others are for my personal use, and were created, so to speak, out of "boredom," however, their execution allowed me to develop significantly. Below is also a set of certificates that show the process of my development over the years, not only in my main field of interest.

    My goal for the future is not only to use available solutions, but also to contribute to the geospatial community with my own ideas.
